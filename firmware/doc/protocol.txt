The device works with single-byte messages.

Request-reply command:

 <bs> - echo signature: "lite"

Input commands:

 P - turn power on
 p - turn power off
 L - turn light on (works only if power is on)
 l - turn light off

Output events:

 P - power turned on
 p - power turned off
 L - light turned on
 l - light turned off
